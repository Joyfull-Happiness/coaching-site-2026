The Micro-Action Method

The core framework behind everything I teach. Three shifts that break the start-stop cycle for good.

01

Shrink The Task

If it can't be done in 15 minutes with "good enough" as the standard, it's too big. Perfectionism is the architect of oversized tasks. Fire it.

02

Protect The Streak

Consistency over correctness. A 12-minute imperfect action done daily beats a 3-hour perfect session done once a month. Every time.

03

Let The Habit Lead

Do it daily until it stops being something you're trying to do and starts being who you are. That's the identity shift. That's the real work.
