About The Show

Transformation to Expansion started as a weekly live stream — just me, a mic, and real conversations about what it actually takes to change your life. Not the highlight reel. The messy middle.

Every episode explores the intersection of mental health, purpose, spirituality, and action. Whether it's a solo pep talk about the start-stop cycle, a deep-dive with a guest on channeling and consciousness, or practical frameworks for career transitions — this show is for tech professionals who are done consuming and ready to start creating.

New episodes drop weekly. Subscribe wherever you listen.
