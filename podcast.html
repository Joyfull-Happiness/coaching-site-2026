About The Show

Transformation to Expansion is a spiritual interview podcast hosted by Lajoie Bradley, featuring 100+ conversations with spiritual leaders, healers, shamans, angel experts, energy intuitives, and teachers from around the world — with a deep focus on the voices of female spiritual leaders.

Each episode is an invitation into someone's full transformation story — the techniques they use, the moments that cracked them open, and the wisdom they now carry. From dream interpretation and guardian angels to frequency healing and unlocking the subconscious, this is where seekers come to expand.

New episodes drop weekly. Listen wherever you are.
